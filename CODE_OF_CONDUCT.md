# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers of the **Expense Tracker Application**, pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, or level of experience.

We are committed to fostering a welcoming environment that encourages collaboration, learning, and mutual respect.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive behavior includes:
- Being respectful and considerate in discussions
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Using inclusive and professional language
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences

### Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Abusive, threatening, or inappropriate language
- Any behavior that would be considered unprofessional in a collaborative environment

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Reviewing and addressing reported issues responsibly
- Removing or rejecting contributions that violate this policy

Maintainers have the right to moderate comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Any interactions related to this project, both online and offline
